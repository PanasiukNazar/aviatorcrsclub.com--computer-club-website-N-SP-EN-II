Cookie Policy

Introduction

At Aviator Computer Club, we value your privacy and aim to provide you with the best experience when browsing our website. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our site. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. These cookies help us remember your preferences, improve website functionality, and analyze your browsing behavior.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features such as navigation, security, and session management. Without these cookies, the website would not function as intended.

2. Performance Cookies

Performance cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. They collect data such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and any issues encountered during your browsing experience. This information helps us improve the performance and functionality of our website.

3. Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and provide a more personalized experience. For example, they help us remember your language preferences, and any settings you’ve selected during previous visits.

4. Advertising and Tracking Cookies

Advertising cookies are used to display personalized advertisements that are relevant to you. They track your browsing habits and help us offer content tailored to your interests.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several reasons:

  • To improve user experience on our website
  • To analyze trends and gather demographic information
  • To personalize content and advertising
  • To manage website performance

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the option to control the cookies stored on your device through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to manage cookie preferences, such as disabling or deleting cookies. However, please note that some features of our website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Third-Party Cookies

In some cases, we may use third-party services that set cookies on our website. These third parties help us with analytics, advertising, and content delivery. We do not have control over these cookies and encourage you to review the privacy and cookie policies of these third parties for more information.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review the policy regularly. Continued use of our website after any changes to this policy signifies your acceptance of the updated terms.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Cookie Policy, or if you would like more information on how we use cookies, please contact us. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is respected and that your experience on our website is secure.
